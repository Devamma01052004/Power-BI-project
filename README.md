# 📊 Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard analyzes sales data to provide insights into revenue, profit, customer segments, and product performance.

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful visual insights that help understand business performance and identify key trends.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall **sales and profitability**
* Identify **top-performing product categories**
* Track **sales trends over time**
* Understand **regional and segment-wise sales distribution**
* Visualize business performance using interactive dashboards

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard includes five major KPIs:

* **Total Sales** – Total revenue generated
* **Total Profit** – Overall profit from sales
* **Total Orders** – Number of unique orders placed
* **Total Quantity Sold** – Total products sold
* **Profit Margin (%)** – Profit as a percentage of total sales

These KPIs provide a quick overview of business performance.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

The dashboard includes the following charts:

* **Line Chart** – Sales Trend Over Time
* **Area Chart** – Profit Trend Over Time
* **Bar Chart** – Sales by Product Category
* **Clustered Bar Chart** – Profit by Sub-Category
* **Pie Chart** – Sales Distribution by Customer Segment
* **Donut Chart** – Regional Sales Contribution
* **Treemap** – Category and Sub-category Sales Contribution
* **Funnel Chart** – Sales Contribution by Category

These visuals help identify patterns in sales, profit, and customer behavior.
